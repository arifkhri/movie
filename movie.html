The Best Movies According to Ari

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Jumbo

A touching story about a young woman who forms an unusual bond with a fairground ride, blending emotion and imagination.

Spiderman Universe

An innovative animated superhero film with stunning visuals, multiple Spider-heroes, and a heartfelt coming-of-age story.

Doraemon

A beloved Japanese classic about a robotic cat from the future who helps Nobita with futuristic gadgets and life lessons.
